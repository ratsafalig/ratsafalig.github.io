<div class="article__content" itemprop="articleBody"><h1 id="自动配置">自动配置</h1>

<p>Spring配置的核心在于几个注解:</p>

<div class="language-java highlighter-rouge"><div class="highlight"><pre class="highlight"><code><span class="nd">@Configuration</span>
<span class="nd">@Import</span>

<span class="nd">@ConditionalOnClass</span>
<span class="nd">@ConditionalOnMissingClass</span>

<span class="nd">@ConditionalOnBean</span>
<span class="nd">@ConditionalOnMissingBean</span>

<span class="nd">@ConditionalOnProperty</span>
<span class="nd">@ConditionalOnMissingProperty</span>

<span class="nd">@AutoConfigureOrder</span>
<span class="nd">@AutoConfigureAfter</span>
</code></pre></div></div>

<p>通常库的自动配置遵循这样一种惯例(不是强制),例如:</p>

<blockquote>

  <ul>
    <li>WebMvcAutoConfiguration :  spring-mvc</li>
    <li>MybatisAutoConfiguration : mybatis</li>
    <li>…</li>
  </ul>
</blockquote>
